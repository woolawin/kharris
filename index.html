About

Kerri-Anne is a Occupational Therapist who has worked across a range of paediatric settings for over 13 years, before starting private work in 2023. Currently all appointments are home visits within Ipswich (and visiting Lockyer Valley once a fortnight), however some clinic appointments will soon be available. Kerri-Anne holds current registration with the Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency (AHPRA). Kerri-Anne has three children herself, aged between 4 and 12.

Services

Providing Occupational Therapy for children needing support with:
  • Self-care skills (toileting, mealtimes, bedtime, dressing, hygiene such as brushing teeth and bathing, etc)
  • Play
  • Learning
  • Fine motor skills
  • Pre-writing development and handwriting
  • Social interactions
  • Sensory processing and participation in home/educational/community environments
  • Regulation (emotional and behavioural)
